# Partnership Brief — ZyraLabs (Kana)

## 1. Opportunity
- **Product**: Chota Crypto, a micro-investing app turning UPI deposits into curated cross-chain packs.
- **Market**: India’s 110M retail investors; UPI volumes >₹15L Cr/month.
- **Need**: Reliable cross-chain routing, gasless UX, on/off ramps without overwhelming users.

## 2. Why ZyraLabs
- Proven infra: $10B+ swap volume, 60K MAU, Paymaster sponsoring ~1M tx/day (per rebrand note `https://xyralabs.medium.com/...`).
- Feature fit: AMM DEX Aggregator across 9+ chains, Mirai smart wallet, Paymaster, plug-in widget.
- On-ramp adjacency: Petra Wallet already integrates Stripe/Banxa/Mesh/Transak, matching our INR requirements.

## 3. What We Need
1. **SDK Access**: Aggregator + Mirai wallet + Paymaster (Aptos + EVM).
2. **Custom Routes**: Ability to predefine pack portfolios and reuse intent templates.
3. **Co-marketing**: Joint PR on “mutual-fund style DeFi for India.”
4. **Support SLA**: 99.9% uptime, incident escalation under 15 min.

## 4. Value to ZyraLabs
- Tap into mass retail onboarding (projected 200K users in year 1) with recurring SIP flows.
- Showcase Zyra as infrastructure for compliant, fiat-facing apps.
- Revenue share on swap spreads + potential Paymaster usage fees.

## 5. Next Steps
- Week 1: Tech workshop (pack orchestration, SDK integration checklist).
- Week 2: Sandbox build using Kana widget + INR test data.
- Week 3: Joint GTM plan, define KPIs (SIP count, routed volume, Paymaster usage).
- Week 4: Sign commercial + data processing agreements.
